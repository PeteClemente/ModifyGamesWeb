We Make Games
We'd Want to Play

Two brothers, a group of close friends, and a deep love of interactive worlds — that's ModifyGames. We build with passion, ship with care, and play with purpose.

Our Mission

"Modify Games creates imaginative, mechanics-driven experiences that push the boundaries of interactive storytelling. We craft games that challenge the mind, stir the heart, and invite players to reflect, connect, and grow."

Our Story

Born from Late Nights
and Long Sessions

ModifyGames started the way most great things do — around a screen, with controllers in hand, arguing about game design at two in the morning. Two brothers who grew up dissecting every game they played, talking about what made them work, what made them feel, and what could make them even better.

Over time, those conversations turned into prototypes. Prototypes turned into projects. And projects, with the help of close friends who shared the same obsession, turned into a studio.

We're not chasing trends. We're building the games we've always wanted to play — each one driven by a core mechanic that genuinely excites us, wrapped in a world that feels worth exploring.

What We Stand For

🎯 Mechanics First

Every game starts with a feeling — something that's fun to do before anything else. We nail that before building everything around it.

💡 Creative Courage

We take genuine risks with genre, tone, and design. Playing it safe doesn't make memorable games.

❤️ Made with Care

Small team, big intention. Every detail in every game gets considered. We'd rather ship something great late than something average on time.

🎮 Players First

We make games for people who love games. Our community matters to us, and we listen to it.
